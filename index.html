About Our Association

Miyetti Allah Kautal Hore is the premier socio-cultural organization representing the Fulani people across Nigeria. Founded to preserve our rich cultural heritage, we serve as a unifying platform for all Fulani communities.

Our organization works tirelessly to promote peaceful coexistence between herders and farmers, while advocating for the rights and welfare of pastoralists nationwide.

Our Aims & Objectives

  1. To preserve and promote Fulani socio-cultural heritage.
  2. To seek and carter for the social welfare of its members with a view to enhancing the status, respect and honor of Fulani race.
  3. To have a body which unite all Fulani traditional rulers, their subjects and kith and kin.
  4. To inculcate in its membersâ€™ the spirit of showing undiluted loyalty and respect for Fulani traditional rulers and other traditional institutions.
  5. To liaise with other associations with similar objectives towards moving Nigeria forward.
  6. To foster good neighborliness and socio-cultural co-operation among our communities.
  7. To encourage and assist our members towards self-actualization.
  8. To encourage educational development of the Fulani pastoralist.
  9. To help in propagating the culture and tradition of the Fulani particularly via the promotion of Fulani language and customs.
  10. To contribute to the development of projects in Fulani communities.
  11. To promote peaceful rearing of cattle and peaceful co-existence by its members in every community in Nigeria.
  12. xii. To give traditional and cultural titles to members and non-members who distinguished themselves in national assignments especially in areas that positively affects the association.

Our Key Initiatives

Education

Nomadic education programs and scholarships for Fulani youth across Nigeria.

Conflict Resolution

Mediation programs to promote peaceful coexistence between herders and farmers.

Cultural Preservation

Documenting and teaching traditional Fulani music, dance, and oral traditions.
