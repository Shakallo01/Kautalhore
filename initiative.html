Our Initiatives

Transforming lives and communities through purposeful programs

Making a Difference

Miyetti Allah Kautal Hore is committed to improving the lives of pastoral communities across Nigeria through targeted initiatives that address education, healthcare, conflict resolution, and economic empowerment. Our programs are designed to preserve our cultural heritage while adapting to the challenges of the modern world.

Nomadic Education Program

Our innovative education initiative brings schooling to pastoral communities through mobile classrooms and flexible schedules that accommodate traditional migration patterns. We provide:

  • Mobile schools that follow grazing routes
  • Bilingual education in Fulfulde and English
  • Vocational training for older students
  • Scholarships for exceptional students

Pastoral Healthcare Initiative

Our mobile health clinics deliver essential medical services to remote pastoral communities, addressing the unique health challenges faced by nomadic populations.

Services include:

  • Vaccination programs for children and livestock
  • Maternal and child health services
  • Treatment of tropical diseases
  • Health education workshops

Peacebuilding & Conflict Resolution

We facilitate dialogue and mediation between herders and farmers to prevent and resolve conflicts over land and resources. Our approach includes:

  • Community peace committees
  • Conflict early warning systems
  • Mediation training for traditional leaders
  • Joint resource management programs
